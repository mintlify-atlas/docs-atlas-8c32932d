## Overview

The Trustpilot integration extends RatingFlow's smart-routing capabilities to one of the world's most trusted review platforms. By connecting with Trustpilot, you can build credibility with potential customers who value verified, independent reviews.

<Note>
Trustpilot is particularly valuable for e-commerce businesses, B2B services, and companies targeting international markets.
</Note>

## How it works

RatingFlow's Trustpilot integration uses the same intelligent filtering system that protects your reputation across all platforms:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Customer receives review request">
    After a transaction or service interaction, your customer receives a review request via SMS, email, or QR code.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Pre-screening on neutral page">
    The customer rates their experience on a private RatingFlow page before being directed to any public review platform.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Intelligent routing">
    If the customer rates their experience 4 or 5 stars, they're automatically redirected to your Trustpilot profile to leave a public review.
    
    If the rating is 1-3 stars, they're directed to a private feedback form where you can address their concerns directly without public exposure.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Why integrate with Trustpilot

### Build international credibility

Trustpilot is recognized globally as a trusted source of customer reviews. A strong Trustpilot profile can significantly boost credibility with international customers.

### Stand out from competitors

Many businesses don't actively manage their Trustpilot presence. By using RatingFlow to systematically collect positive reviews, you can differentiate yourself from competitors.

### Display verified reviews

Trustpilot's verification system adds an extra layer of authenticity to your reviews, making them more persuasive to potential customers.

### Increase conversion rates

Studies show that Trustpilot reviews significantly influence purchasing decisions, especially for online transactions and high-consideration purchases.

## Setting up your Trustpilot integration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create or claim your Trustpilot profile">
    Ensure you have a verified business profile on Trustpilot and necessary access credentials.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Connect to RatingFlow">
    Link your Trustpilot account to RatingFlow using your profile URL or API credentials.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure your review funnel">
    Set the star rating threshold (typically 4-5 stars) that triggers redirection to Trustpilot.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Customize request templates">
    Create personalized SMS and email templates explaining the value of Trustpilot reviews.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Begin collecting reviews">
    Start your automated review request campaigns and build your Trustpilot reputation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

### Timing your requests

Send Trustpilot review requests at the optimal moment:

- **E-commerce**: After product delivery and customer has time to evaluate
- **Services**: Immediately after project completion or service delivery
- **B2B**: After key milestones or successful outcomes

### Segment your approach

Consider directing different customer segments to different platforms:

- International customers → Trustpilot
- Local customers → Google Maps
- Social media engaged customers → Facebook

<Note>
RatingFlow allows you to configure multiple platform integrations and route customers based on your business logic.
</Note>

## Automated review collection

Stop manually chasing customers for Trustpilot reviews. RatingFlow automates the entire process:

- **Bulk SMS campaigns**: Send review requests to multiple customers efficiently
- **Email automation**: Set up triggered sequences based on customer actions
- **QR codes**: Generate codes for packaging inserts or receipts that can route to Trustpilot

## Protect your brand reputation

The Trustpilot integration includes RatingFlow's signature negative feedback shield:

- **Filter before publication**: Catch dissatisfied customers before they leave public negative reviews
- **Private resolution channel**: Address complaints directly and save the customer relationship
- **Maintain high ratings**: Keep your Trustpilot score high by ensuring your best customers become public advocates

<Note>
Businesses that actively manage their Trustpilot reputation see higher conversion rates and increased customer trust compared to those with unmanaged or low-rated profiles.
</Note>

## Multi-platform strategy

The Trustpilot integration works seamlessly alongside RatingFlow's Google Maps and Facebook integrations, allowing you to build a comprehensive online reputation across all major platforms. Configure routing rules to determine which satisfied customers are sent to which platform based on your business priorities.
