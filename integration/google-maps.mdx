## Overview

The Google Maps integration is a core feature of RatingFlow's 5-star review generator. By connecting your business to Google Maps, you can automatically redirect satisfied customers to leave public reviews, improving your local search visibility and attracting more customers.

<Note>
Businesses with higher review volumes and star ratings rank higher in Google's Local Pack and Maps results.
</Note>

## How it works

RatingFlow uses a smart-routing system to filter customer feedback before it becomes public:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Customer receives review request">
    Your customers receive a review request via SMS, email, or QR code after interacting with your business.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Customer rates their experience">
    Customers provide an initial rating on a private RatingFlow page before being directed to any public platform.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Smart routing based on rating">
    If the customer rates their experience 4 or 5 stars, they're instantly redirected to your Google Maps listing to leave a public review.
    
    If the rating is 1-3 stars, they're directed to a private feedback form instead, protecting your public reputation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Benefits of Google Maps integration

### Boost local SEO

A higher volume of positive Google reviews directly correlates to improved rankings in Google's Local Pack and Maps search results. This means more visibility when potential customers search for businesses like yours.

### Automate review collection

Stop manually chasing customers for reviews. RatingFlow automates the entire process, sending requests and routing satisfied customers directly to your Google Maps listing.

### Protect your brand

By filtering negative feedback before it reaches Google, you maintain control over your public reputation while still gathering valuable insights from dissatisfied customers.

## Setting up your Google Maps integration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your Google Business Profile">
    Link your verified Google Business Profile to RatingFlow. This ensures customers are directed to the correct listing.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure your review funnel">
    Set the star rating threshold (typically 4-5 stars) that triggers redirection to Google Maps.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Customize your request templates">
    Create personalized SMS and email templates that encourage customers to share their experience.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start sending requests">
    Begin sending automated review requests to your customers through your preferred channels.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Make sure your Google Business Profile is verified and up-to-date before connecting it to RatingFlow.
</Note>

## Best practices

- **Timing matters**: Send review requests shortly after a positive customer interaction for the best response rates
- **Keep it simple**: Make the review process as frictionless as possible with direct links and clear instructions
- **Follow up strategically**: Use automated follow-up sequences for customers who don't respond to the first request
- **Monitor your results**: Track which request methods (SMS, email, QR) generate the most reviews for your business

## Impact on revenue

Businesses with a 4.5+ star rating on Google Maps generate significantly more revenue than competitors with lower ratings. The Google Maps integration helps you achieve and maintain high ratings by ensuring your best customers become your public advocates.
