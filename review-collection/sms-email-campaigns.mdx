## Overview

Stop chasing clients manually for reviews. RatingFlow allows you to send bulk review requests via SMS and email, streamlining the process to get more Google reviews with zero friction.

By automating your outreach, you can consistently gather feedback from satisfied customers without the tedious work of individual follow-ups.

## How SMS and email campaigns work

RatingFlow's automated review request system operates on a smart-routing principle:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Send review requests">
    Send bulk SMS or email requests to your customer list with personalized review links.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Customer rates their experience">
    Recipients click the link and provide a star rating before being directed to any public platform.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Smart routing based on sentiment">
    - **4-5 star ratings:** Customers are instantly redirected to public platforms like Google Maps, Facebook, or Trustpilot
    - **1-3 star ratings:** Customers are diverted to a private internal feedback form for direct resolution
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The smart-routing system acts as a shield for your business, ensuring satisfied customers publicly advocate for your brand while unsatisfied customers are routed to a private resolution channel.
</Note>

## Best practices

<Tip>
  Send review requests within 24-48 hours of a customer interaction when their experience is still fresh in their mind.
</Tip>

- **Personalize your messages** with the customer's name and specific service details
- **Keep it brief** - make it easy for customers to understand what you're asking
- **Time it right** - avoid sending requests during holidays or late at night
- **Follow up strategically** - send a gentle reminder to non-responders after a few days
