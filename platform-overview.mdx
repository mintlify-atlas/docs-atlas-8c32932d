## How RatingFlow works

RatingFlow is built on a smart-routing system that segments customer sentiment before it reaches public review platforms. This architecture protects your brand reputation while maximizing positive social proof.

## The smart-routing workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review request delivery">
    You deliver review requests to customers through multiple channels:
    
    - **SMS/Text messages**: Automated bulk or individual text messages with a direct link
    - **Email campaigns**: Branded email requests sent from your domain
    - **QR codes**: Scannable codes placed at your physical location
    
    All methods direct customers to a private RatingFlow landing page—not directly to Google or other public platforms.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Private sentiment capture">
    When customers click your review link or scan your QR code, they land on a branded page where they rate their experience privately.
    
    This is the critical filter layer:
    
    <Info>
      Customers never see Google, Facebook, or any public platform at this stage. They simply provide an honest rating in a neutral environment.
    </Info>
    
    Rating options include:
    - Star ratings (1-5 stars)
    - Numeric scores (1-10)
    - Emoji feedback (happy, neutral, sad)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Intelligent routing decision">
    Based on the customer's rating, RatingFlow automatically routes them to one of two paths:
    
    ### 5 star reviews generator (positive path)
    
    **Customers who rate 4-5 stars** are immediately redirected to public review platforms:
    
    - Google Maps/Google Business Profile
    - Facebook Business Page
    - Trustpilot
    - Yelp (if applicable)
    - Industry-specific platforms
    
    <Tip>
      This automated redirect to public platforms is what makes RatingFlow a powerful **5 star reviews generator**. Happy customers are guided to leave public reviews with minimal friction.
    </Tip>
    
    ### Negative feedback shield (protection path)
    
    **Customers who rate 1-3 stars** are redirected to a private feedback form instead:
    
    - They never reach public platforms
    - They can describe their concerns in detail
    - You receive an immediate notification
    - You can respond directly and resolve the issue
    - The negative sentiment never impacts your public rating
    
    <Warning>
      This doesn't suppress legitimate reviews—it gives you a chance to fix problems before they become permanent public damage.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Dashboard monitoring and response">
    All activity flows into your RatingFlow dashboard:
    
    - Real-time notification of new reviews (both public and private)
    - Analytics on request delivery, open rates, and response rates
    - Private feedback management system
    - Public review tracking from all connected platforms
    - Performance metrics by campaign, time period, or channel
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Platform architecture

RatingFlow's architecture is designed for scale, reliability, and ease of use:

### Core components

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Review request engine" icon="paper-plane">
    Handles SMS, email, and QR code generation. Manages delivery, scheduling, and tracking.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Landing page builder" icon="browser">
    Creates branded, mobile-optimized review pages. Captures sentiment before public routing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Smart router" icon="route">
    Routes customers to public platforms or private forms based on configurable rating thresholds.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Feedback management system" icon="comments">
    Collects, organizes, and alerts you to private feedback. Enables direct customer communication.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Analytics engine" icon="chart-mixed">
    Tracks all metrics across campaigns. Provides insights into review volume, sentiment trends, and ROI.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Widget generator" icon="code">
    Creates embeddable widgets that display your best reviews on your website.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Integration points

RatingFlow integrates with key platforms to maximize your reputation management:

- **Review platforms**: Google My Business, Facebook, Trustpilot, Yelp
- **Communication channels**: Twilio (SMS), SendGrid (Email), QR code generation
- **CRM systems**: Import customer data from popular CRM platforms
- **Website embedding**: JavaScript widgets for displaying reviews

## Security and compliance

RatingFlow is built with security and compliance in mind:

- **Data encryption**: All customer data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **GDPR compliance**: Full data privacy controls and right-to-deletion
- **CAN-SPAM compliance**: Automatic opt-out handling for email requests
- **TCPA compliance**: SMS consent tracking and opt-out management

<Warning>
  Always ensure you have permission to contact customers before sending review requests. RatingFlow provides the tools, but compliance is your responsibility.
</Warning>

## Performance and reliability

- **99.9% uptime**: Enterprise-grade infrastructure ensures your review requests always get delivered
- **Global delivery**: SMS and email sent through tier-1 providers with worldwide reach
- **Mobile-optimized**: All landing pages are lightning-fast and work perfectly on any device
- **Real-time processing**: Reviews are routed instantly—no delays between rating and redirect
