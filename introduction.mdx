## What is RatingFlow?

RatingFlow is a leading online reputation management platform designed to help local businesses, agencies, and service providers automate the collection of positive feedback while protecting your brand from public negativity.

As a powerful Google review management software, RatingFlow solves the critical issue of low review volume and the disproportionate impact of negative ratings. By creating a seamless digital funnel, the platform acts as a shield for your business, ensuring that satisfied customers publicly advocate for your brand while unsatisfied customers are routed to a private resolution channel.

## How it works

RatingFlow operates on a smart-routing system that segments customer sentiment before it goes public:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Customer receives review request">
    You send automated review requests via SMS, email, or customers scan a QR code at your location.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer rates their experience">
    Customers select a star rating (1-5) on a private landing page before being directed anywhere public.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Smart routing based on rating">
    Happy customers (4-5 stars) are redirected to public platforms like Google, Facebook, or Trustpilot. Unsatisfied customers (1-3 stars) are routed to a private feedback form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Protect and grow your reputation">
    Public reviews boost your SEO and social proof, while private feedback gives you a chance to resolve issues directly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
