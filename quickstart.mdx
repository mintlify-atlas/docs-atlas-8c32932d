Get your RatingFlow account set up and send your first review request in under 10 minutes. This guide walks you through the essential steps to start collecting 5-star reviews and protecting your brand reputation.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A RatingFlow account (sign up at [ratingflow.co](https://ratingflow.co))
- Your Google Business Profile or other review platform credentials
- A list of customer contact information (email addresses or phone numbers)

## Set up your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up for RatingFlow and verify your email address. Choose the plan that fits your business size and needs.
    
    <Tip>
    Agencies should select the white label plan to rebrand the platform for your clients.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure your business profile">
    Add your business information including:
    - Business name and logo
    - Industry and location
    - Review platform links (Google Maps, Facebook, Trustpilot)
    - Contact preferences
    
    <Info>
    Make sure your Google Business Profile link is accurate — this is where your 5-star reviews will be directed.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set up sentiment routing">
    Configure the smart-routing system that protects your brand:
    
    - **High ratings (4-5 stars)**: Direct to public review platforms
    - **Low ratings (1-3 stars)**: Route to private feedback form
    
    Customize the rating threshold and feedback form fields to match your business needs.
    
    <Warning>
    Test your routing flow before sending to customers. Click "Preview" to see both the positive and negative customer journeys.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Customize your messaging">
    Personalize the review request messages sent to your customers:
    
    - Email subject lines and body text
    - SMS message templates
    - Thank you page content
    - Follow-up sequences
    
    Use merge tags like `{customer_name}` and `{business_name}` to personalize each request.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Send your first review request

Choose the method that works best for your business:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="SMS/Email campaign">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Upload your customer list">
        Import contacts via CSV upload or manual entry. Include customer name, email, and/or phone number.
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Select your audience">
        Choose who receives the review request:
        - All customers
        - Recent purchases only
        - High-value customers
        - Custom segment
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Choose your channel">
        Send via SMS, email, or both for maximum response rates.
        
        <Tip>
        SMS typically gets 3-5x higher open rates than email, but email allows for more detailed messaging.
        </Tip>
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Schedule or send immediately">
        Launch your campaign immediately or schedule it for optimal timing (avoid weekends and late nights).
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="QR code">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Generate your QR code">
        Navigate to **QR Codes** in your dashboard and click **Create New QR Code**. Select your review routing settings.
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Download and print">
        Download the QR code image in high resolution. Print it on:
        - Receipts
        - Table tents
        - Business cards
        - Window decals
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Place strategically">
        Position QR codes where customers can easily scan them:
        - At checkout counters
        - On receipts
        - Near exits
        - On product packaging
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    
    <Info>
    Each QR code includes your smart-routing system — customers see the rating screen first, then get directed based on their sentiment.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
