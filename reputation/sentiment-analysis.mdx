RatingFlow uses intelligent sentiment analysis to automatically route customer feedback to the right destination—positive experiences go to public review platforms while negative experiences are directed to private resolution channels.

## The smart-routing system

At the core of RatingFlow is a sentiment detection engine that segments customers based on their rating before any feedback becomes public.

<Info>
  Sentiment routing happens in real-time, creating a seamless experience for customers while protecting your brand reputation.
</Info>

## How sentiment routing works

When a customer responds to your review request, RatingFlow instantly evaluates their sentiment:

### High satisfaction (4-5 stars)

Customers who rate their experience as 4 or 5 stars are automatically identified as satisfied and redirected to public review platforms.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Positive rating detected">
    Customer selects 4 or 5 stars in response to your review request.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Public platform redirect">
    Customer is instantly redirected to your chosen public platform (Google Maps, Facebook, Trustpilot, etc.).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review submission">
    Customer completes their positive review on the public platform, boosting your star rating and social proof.
  </Step>
</Steps>

This automated flow acts as a **5 star reviews generator**, driving positive feedback to the platforms that matter most for your business.

### Low satisfaction (1-3 stars)

Customers who rate their experience as 1, 2, or 3 stars are identified as dissatisfied and redirected to a private feedback channel.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Negative rating detected">
    Customer selects 1, 2, or 3 stars in response to your review request.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Private form redirect">
    Customer is redirected to an internal feedback form instead of a public review platform.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Private feedback submission">
    Customer explains their concerns in the private form, which goes directly to you.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Resolution opportunity">
    You receive the feedback and can address the issue directly with the customer before it impacts your public rating.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  This routing protects your public star rating from permanent damage while still giving dissatisfied customers a voice.
</Warning>

## Benefits of sentiment-based routing

By segmenting feedback based on satisfaction levels, you gain multiple advantages:

### Automated social proof growth

You don't need to manually identify and encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews—the system does it automatically, ensuring a steady flow of positive public feedback.

### Brand protection

Negative experiences are captured and addressed privately before they can permanently damage your online reputation.

### Local SEO optimization

Higher volumes of positive reviews correlate directly to better rankings in Google's "Local Pack" and Google Maps search results.

### Customer relationship management

Private feedback gives you the opportunity to recover relationships with dissatisfied customers, potentially turning detractors into advocates.

<Info>
  Businesses with a 4.5+ star rating generate significantly more revenue than competitors with lower ratings.
</Info>

## Multi-platform support

Sentiment routing works across all major review platforms:

- **Google Maps**: Drive positive reviews to improve local search visibility
- **Facebook**: Build social proof on your business page
- **Trustpilot**: Enhance credibility for e-commerce and service businesses
- **Industry-specific platforms**: Route to platforms relevant to your niche

You can configure which platform positive reviews are sent to based on your business priorities.

## The customer experience

From the customer's perspective, sentiment routing is seamless:

1. They receive a review request via SMS, email, or scan a QR code
2. They select their rating on a simple interface
3. They're immediately taken to the appropriate destination
4. They complete their feedback or review

<Info>
  Customers experience a smooth, frictionless process regardless of their satisfaction level—there's no indication that routing is happening behind the scenes.
</Info>

## Customization options

You can adjust sentiment thresholds based on your business needs:

- **Conservative approach**: Only route 5-star ratings to public platforms (4 stars go private)
- **Balanced approach**: Route 4-5 stars publicly, 1-3 stars privately (default)
- **Aggressive approach**: Only route 1-2 stars privately, all others go public

<Warning>
  Choose your threshold carefully. Being too aggressive may allow lukewarm 3-star reviews to become public, which can still drag down your average rating.
</Warning>
