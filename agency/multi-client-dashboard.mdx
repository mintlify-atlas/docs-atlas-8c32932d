## Manage review campaigns at scale

You can automate the collection of positive feedback across all your clients while protecting their brands from public negativity. The dashboard segments customer sentiment before it goes public:

- **5 star reviews generator**: Customers who rate their experience highly (4-5 stars) are instantly redirected to public platforms like Google Maps, Facebook, or Trustpilot
- **Negative feedback shield**: Customers who report a negative experience (1-3 stars) are diverted to a private internal form for direct resolution

<Note>
  This smart-routing system helps boost local SEO rankings and protect brand integrity for all your clients simultaneously.
</Note>

Stop chasing clients manually. The multi-client dashboard lets you:

- Send bulk review requests via **SMS/Text** and **Email**
- Schedule automated follow-ups
- Track delivery and response rates
- Customize messaging per client or campaign

<Info>
  Streamline the process to get more Google reviews with zero friction across your entire client base.

## QR code management

Generate and distribute custom QR codes for all your clients from the central dashboard. Perfect for physical businesses like:

- Restaurants and cafes
- Hotels and hospitality
- Retail stores
- Healthcare practices
- Real estate offices

Customers can scan and review instantly on their mobile devices, making it easy to collect feedback at the point of service.

Deploy embeddable widgets across all your client websites to showcase their success. Display the latest 4 and 5-star ratings directly on their sites to increase conversion rates and build trust with potential buyers.
