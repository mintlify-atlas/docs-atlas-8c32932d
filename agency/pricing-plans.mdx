## Key features by tier

As you grow your agency and take on more clients, you can upgrade to unlock additional capabilities:

### Agency starter

Perfect for boutique agencies or those just starting to offer reputation management services.

- Multi-client dashboard
- White label branding (logo, colors, domain)
- Automated SMS and email campaigns
- QR code management
- Basic analytics and reporting
- Email support

### Agency professional

Designed for established agencies managing medium-sized client portfolios.

- Everything in Agency starter, plus:
- Advanced cross-client analytics
- Custom reporting templates
- Branded client portals
- API access for integrations
- Priority email and chat support
- Dedicated account manager

### Agency enterprise

Built for large agencies and resellers requiring maximum flexibility and support.

- Everything in Agency professional, plus:
- Unlimited client accounts
- Custom feature development
- White glove onboarding
- SLA guarantees
- Phone support
- Quarterly business reviews

<Note>
  Enterprise plans can be customized to meet your specific agency needs and client volume.
</Note>

When you offer RatingFlow to your clients, you're helping them achieve measurable business outcomes:

- **Boost local SEO**: Higher volume of positive reviews directly correlates to higher rankings in the Local Pack and Google Maps
