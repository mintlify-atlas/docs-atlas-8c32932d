## What's included

As a RatingFlow reseller, you get access to our complete platform with full white label capabilities:

### Platform features

- **5 star reviews generator**: Automatically route 4-5 star customers to public platforms like Google Maps, Facebook, and Trustpilot
- **Negative feedback shield**: Divert 1-3 star feedback to private internal forms for direct resolution
- **Automated review requests**: Send bulk requests via SMS/Text and Email to get more Google reviews
- **QR code management**: Generate custom QR codes for physical locations
- **Website widgets**: Embeddable social proof widgets displaying 4 and 5-star ratings
- **Multi-client dashboard**: Manage all clients from a single unified interface

## Reseller benefits

Partner with RatingFlow and deliver measurable results to your clients:

### Boost local SEO for clients

A higher volume of positive reviews directly correlates to higher rankings in the Local Pack and Google Maps. Help your clients dominate local search results.

<Note>
  Businesses that consistently generate 4-5 star reviews see significant improvements in local search visibility within 60-90 days.
</Note>

### Protect brand integrity

Filter out unfair or emotional 1-star reviews before they become permanent. Give your clients the opportunity to resolve complaints privately and save customer relationships.

### Increase client revenue

Businesses with a 4.5+ star rating generate significantly more revenue than competitors with lower ratings. Position yourself as a revenue-driving partner, not just a service provider.
