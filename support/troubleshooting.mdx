## Review requests

### Customers aren't receiving review requests

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify contact information">
    Ensure that customer email addresses and phone numbers are entered correctly in your system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check spam folders">
    Ask customers to check their spam or junk email folders. Add instructions to whitelist emails from RatingFlow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm SMS delivery">
    If using SMS/Text requests, verify that the phone numbers include the correct country code and are in a valid format.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review sending limits">
    Check if you've reached your plan's monthly sending limit for review requests.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Bulk review requests via SMS and Email are designed to streamline the process of getting more Google reviews. If you're experiencing consistent delivery issues, contact support.
</Note>

### Review routing not working correctly

**Issue:** Negative reviews (1-3 stars) are going to public platforms instead of the private form.

**Solution:** 
- Verify that your smart-routing settings are configured correctly in your dashboard
- Ensure you've set up the threshold for what constitutes a "positive" vs "negative" review
- Test the review flow yourself by submitting test reviews at different star levels

<Warning>
  The smart-routing system is critical for protecting your brand. Always test your configuration after making changes to ensure negative feedback is properly diverted to your private internal form.
</Warning>

## QR codes

### QR code not scanning

**Common causes:**
- QR code image is too small or low resolution
- Poor lighting conditions when scanning
- QR code is damaged or partially obscured

**Solutions:**
- Regenerate the QR code at a higher resolution from your dashboard
- Print QR codes at a minimum size of 2x2 inches for optimal scanning
- Test the QR code with multiple devices before deployment
- Ensure adequate lighting where QR codes are displayed

### QR code directs to wrong platform

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your QR code settings">
    Log into your RatingFlow dashboard and verify which platform each QR code is configured to direct customers to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Regenerate if needed">
    If the configuration is incorrect, update the settings and regenerate the QR code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Replace printed materials">
    Update any printed receipts, table tents, or signage with the new QR code.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Website widgets

### Widget not displaying on website

**Troubleshooting steps:**
1. Verify that the widget embed code is correctly placed in your website's HTML
2. Check that there are no JavaScript errors in your browser console
3. Ensure you have at least one 4 or 5-star review to display
4. Clear your browser cache and reload the page
5. Test on multiple browsers to rule out browser-specific issues

<Info>
  Website widgets showcase your latest 4 and 5-star ratings directly on your website. They're designed to increase conversion rates and build trust with potential customers.
</Info>

### Widget showing outdated reviews

**Solution:** Widgets typically refresh automatically, but you can:
- Check the refresh interval setting in your dashboard
- Force a manual refresh from the widget configuration panel
- Clear your website's cache if using a caching plugin or CDN

## White label features

### Branding not appearing correctly

**For agencies using the white label solution:**
- Verify that all branding assets (logo, colors, domain) are uploaded in the correct format and size
- Clear your browser cache to see updated branding changes
- Check that custom domain DNS settings are properly configured
- Allow up to 24-48 hours for DNS propagation if you've recently set up a custom domain

<Note>
  The white label solution allows digital marketers and SEO agencies to rebrand the entire dashboard and reporting system under their own agency brand.
</Note>

## Performance and access

### Dashboard loading slowly

**Quick fixes:**
- Clear your browser cache and cookies
- Try accessing the dashboard from a different browser
- Check your internet connection speed
- Disable browser extensions that might interfere with the platform

### Can't log in to account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify credentials">
    Double-check that you're using the correct email address and password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset password">
    Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset your password if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser data">
    Clear cookies and cache, then try logging in again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If you still can't access your account, reach out to our support team for assistance.
  </Step>
</Steps>
