## Response times

<Note>
  We strive to respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible. Response times may vary based on your plan level and the complexity of your request.
</Note>

**Typical response times:**
- Critical issues (system down, data loss): Within 2-4 hours
- High priority (feature not working): Within 24 hours
- General questions and support: Within 1-2 business days
- Feature requests and suggestions: Reviewed during monthly planning cycles

## Before contacting support

To help us assist you more quickly, please:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the documentation">
    Review our [FAQ](/support/faq) and [troubleshooting guide](/support/troubleshooting) first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Gather relevant information">
    Note any error messages, screenshots, or specific steps that led to the issue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Identify your account details">
    Have your account email address and company name ready.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the issue clearly">
    Explain what you were trying to do, what happened, and what you expected to happen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The more details you provide, the faster we can identify and resolve your issue.
</Tip>
