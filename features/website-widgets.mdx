## Overview

Showcase your success with embeddable website widgets. Display your latest 4 and 5-star ratings directly on your website to increase conversion rates and trust with potential buyers.

<Note>
  Social proof is one of the most powerful conversion optimization tools. Displaying real customer reviews on your website can significantly increase purchase decisions.
</Note>

## Why use review widgets

Website visitors who see positive reviews from real customers are more likely to convert. Review widgets provide instant credibility and reduce purchase anxiety by showing that others have had positive experiences with your business.

## Widget types

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Review carousel">
    **Rotating testimonials:**
    - Displays multiple reviews in a rotating format
    - Automatically cycles through your best reviews
    - Eye-catching and dynamic
    - Perfect for homepage hero sections
    
    Keeps your testimonials fresh and engaging.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Review grid">
    **Multiple reviews at once:**
    - Shows 4-6 reviews in a grid layout
    - Provides comprehensive social proof
    - Great for dedicated testimonial pages
    - Responsive design for all devices
    
    Demonstrates consistent positive feedback.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Review badge">
    **Compact social proof:**
    - Shows average rating and total review count
    - Minimal footprint on your page
    - Ideal for headers or footers
    - Links to full review page
    
    Subtle but effective trust signal.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Floating widget">
    **Sidebar display:**
    - Stays visible as users scroll
    - Unobtrusive but always present
    - Shows latest reviews in real-time
    - Customizable position
    
    Maintains social proof throughout the browsing experience.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## How to embed widgets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your widget style">
    Select the widget type that best fits your website design and goals from your RatingFlow dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Customize appearance">
    Adjust colors, fonts, and sizing to match your website's branding. Preview changes in real-time.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Filter reviews">
    Choose to display only 4-5 star reviews, specific platforms, or reviews from a certain date range.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Copy embed code">
    Generate and copy the simple embed code snippet from your dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add to your website">
    Paste the code into your website HTML where you want the widget to appear. Most website builders support custom code blocks.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Publish and test">
    Publish your changes and verify the widget displays correctly on all devices.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Customization options

### Visual styling

- **Colors:** Match your brand palette
- **Fonts:** Use your website typography
- **Layout:** Choose between light and dark themes
- **Size:** Adjust dimensions to fit your layout
- **Border radius:** Control corner styling

### Content filters

- **Star rating:** Show only 4-5 star reviews
- **Platform:** Display reviews from specific sources
- **Date range:** Feature recent reviews
- **Keywords:** Highlight reviews mentioning specific topics

<Warning>
  Only displaying 4-5 star reviews on your website is a best practice. Lower-rated reviews can be valuable for learning but may hurt conversion rates when prominently displayed.
</Warning>

## Best placement strategies

### Homepage

Place a review carousel or grid in a prominent position to immediately establish credibility with new visitors.

### Product/service pages

Show relevant reviews near purchase buttons to reduce hesitation at the critical decision moment.

### Landing pages

Include social proof on paid traffic landing pages to maximize conversion rates and justify ad spend.

### About page

Reinforce your story and values with authentic customer testimonials that validate your claims.

### Checkout page

Reduce cart abandonment by displaying trust signals during the purchase process.

## Impact on conversions

Businesses that display customer reviews on their website typically see:

- **15-30% increase in conversion rates**
- **Higher average order values** from increased trust
- **Lower bounce rates** as visitors engage with social proof
- **Improved SEO** from fresh, user-generated content

<Note>
  Websites with visible reviews generate significantly more revenue than competitors without social proof.
</Note>
