## Overview

Stop chasing clients manually. RatingFlow's automated review request system allows you to send bulk requests via SMS/Text and email, making it effortless to get more Google reviews and build your online reputation.

<Note>
  Automated review requests eliminate the manual burden of following up with customers, allowing you to focus on delivering great service while reviews come in automatically.
</Note>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Import your contacts">
    Upload your customer list or integrate with your existing CRM to identify who should receive review requests.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose your channel">
    Select whether to send requests via SMS, email, or both for maximum reach and response rates.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Send bulk requests">
    Launch your campaign with a single click to send review requests to all selected contacts simultaneously.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Track responses">
    Monitor engagement and review completion rates in real-time through your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Communication channels

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="SMS/Text">

    **Benefits:**
    - Instant delivery and high visibility
    - Mobile-optimized experience
    - Higher response rates than email
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Email">

    **Benefits:**
    - More room for personalization
    - Can include rich formatting and images

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Best practices

### Timing matters

Send review requests when the customer experience is fresh in their mind. For service businesses, this typically means within 24-48 hours of service completion.

### Personalize your message

While the process is automated, your message should feel personal. Reference the specific service or product the customer received.

### Keep it simple

Make the review process as frictionless as possible. Your automated requests should include direct links that take customers straight to the review form.

<Warning>
  Avoid sending too many follow-up requests to the same customer. This can be perceived as spam and damage your relationship.
</Warning>

## Impact on your business

**Boost local SEO:** A higher volume of positive reviews directly correlates to higher rankings in the Local Pack and Google Maps.

**Save time:** Automation eliminates the need for manual follow-ups, freeing up hours every week.

**Increase review volume:** Systematic outreach generates significantly more reviews than sporadic manual requests.
