## Overview

RatingFlow's white label solution is designed specifically for digital marketers and SEO agencies. Rebrand the entire dashboard and reporting system to offer high-end reputation management services to your clients under your own agency brand.

<Note>
  White labeling allows you to add a new revenue stream and increase client retention by offering reputation management as a core service.
</Note>

<Tabs>

    - Replace RatingFlow logo with your agency logo

  <Tab title="Communications">

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up your white label account">
    Configure your agency branding, including logo, colors, and domain settings in your RatingFlow white label portal.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add clients">
    Create individual accounts for each client under your white label instance. Each client gets their own branded dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure services">
    Set up reputation management campaigns, review funnels, and reporting for each client based on their needs.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Manage centrally">
    Monitor all client accounts from your agency master dashboard while clients only see their individual branded interface.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Scale your offering">
    Add unlimited clients and grow your reputation management service line without technical overhead.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Pricing and packaging strategies

### Bundle with existing services

Include reputation management as part of your local SEO or social media packages to increase perceived value.

### Tiered pricing

Offer basic, professional, and enterprise reputation management tiers based on review volume and features.

### Performance-based pricing

Charge based on the number of reviews generated or improvement in star rating.

### Monthly retainer

Position reputation management as an ongoing service with monthly monitoring and optimization.
