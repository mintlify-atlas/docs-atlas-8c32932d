## Client management overview

With RatingFlow's white label solution, you can manage multiple clients from a single agency dashboard. Each client receives their own isolated workspace with customized settings while you maintain centralized oversight and control.

<Info>
  Every client account operates independently with its own review campaigns, QR codes, and reporting—all under your agency brand.
</Info>

## Adding a new client

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create client account">
    From your agency dashboard, click **Add New Client** and enter their business information:
    
    - Business name
    - Industry/category
    - Primary location
    - Contact email
    - Phone number
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure review platforms">
    Set up which platforms the client wants to collect reviews on:
    
    - Google Maps/Google Business Profile
    - Facebook
    - Trustpilot
    - Industry-specific platforms
    
    Add the direct URLs where customers should be sent for 4-5 star reviews.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set rating threshold">
    Configure the smart-routing system:
    
    - **High ratings (4-5 stars):** Redirect to public review platforms
    - **Low ratings (1-3 stars):** Route to private feedback form
    
    You can customize the threshold based on client preferences.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Grant client access">
    Decide on access level:
    
    - **Full access:** Client can log in and view their dashboard
    - **Report only:** Client receives automated reports via email
    - **Agency managed:** You manage everything and share reports manually
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Organizing multiple clients

### Client groups

Organize clients into groups based on:
- Industry type (restaurants, healthcare, real estate)
- Service tier (basic, premium, enterprise)
- Geographic region
- Account manager assignment

### Bulk operations

Manage multiple clients efficiently:
- Apply branding updates across all clients
- Send agency-wide announcements
- Export combined reports for all clients
- Update pricing or service tiers in bulk

<Tip>
  Use client tags to quickly filter and find specific accounts in your dashboard.
</Tip>

## Client access levels

You control how much access each client has to their account:

### Full dashboard access

Clients can log in and use all features independently:
- Send review requests
- Generate QR codes
- View analytics
- Manage feedback
- Download reports

### Limited access

Clients can view reports but cannot modify campaigns or settings. You maintain control over review request campaigns and configuration.

### Report-only access

Clients receive automated email reports but have no dashboard login. This is ideal for clients who want a hands-off experience.

## Managing client campaigns

### Automated review requests

Set up automated campaigns for each client:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload customer contacts">
    Import customer email addresses and phone numbers via CSV or integrate with their CRM system.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Customize request message">
    Personalize the review request message with the client's business name and branding.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set sending schedule">
    Choose when and how often to send requests (immediate, daily batches, weekly).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Monitor response rates">
    Track open rates, click rates, and completed reviews for each campaign.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### QR code campaigns

For clients with physical locations, generate QR codes that can be placed on:
- Receipts
- Table tents
- Front desk displays
- Packaging
- Signage

Customers scan the code and are routed through the smart-filtering system based on their rating.

## Handling negative feedback

When customers leave 1-3 star ratings, they're directed to a private feedback form instead of public review sites. This gives your clients the opportunity to:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Address concerns" icon="headset">
    Respond directly to unhappy customers and resolve issues privately.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Save relationships" icon="heart">
    Turn negative experiences into positive outcomes before they damage public reputation.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Prevent public damage" icon="shield">
    Stop unfair or emotional 1-star reviews from becoming permanent.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Gather insights" icon="lightbulb">
    Learn from feedback patterns to improve business operations.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
  Ensure your clients respond to negative feedback promptly. Quick responses show customers their concerns are taken seriously and increase the chance of resolution.
</Warning>

## Client reporting

You can generate and share reports for each client showing:
- Total reviews collected
- Average star rating
- Review sources (Google, Facebook, etc.)
- Negative feedback intercepted
- Month-over-month growth

Reports are fully branded with your agency logo and can be automated to send weekly or monthly.

## Billing and subscriptions

As a white label agency, you control client pricing:
- Set your own monthly fees per client
- Offer tiered packages (basic, professional, enterprise)
- Add setup fees or custom services
- Manage invoicing directly with clients

<Info>
  RatingFlow bills you for the white label platform. You independently bill your clients at your own rates, allowing you to set your own profit margins.
</Info>
