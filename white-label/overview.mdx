## What is RatingFlow white label?

RatingFlow's white label solution enables digital marketers and SEO agencies to offer premium reputation management services to their clients under their own brand. You can rebrand the entire dashboard and reporting system, positioning yourself as a full-service reputation management provider.

<Info>
  White label is designed specifically for agencies managing multiple clients who want to maintain their own brand identity while delivering powerful review management tools.
</Info>

## Key capabilities

### Complete dashboard rebranding

Your clients see your agency branding throughout their entire experience, from login screens to reporting dashboards. The RatingFlow name and logo are replaced with your custom branding.

### Multi-client management

Manage all your clients from a single agency dashboard. Each client gets their own isolated workspace with custom settings, while you maintain oversight and control.

### Custom domain support

Host the platform on your own subdomain (like `reviews.youragency.com`) to maintain complete brand consistency.

### White labeled reporting

All reports, email notifications, and customer-facing materials display your branding, not RatingFlow's.
