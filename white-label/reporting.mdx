## Reporting overview

RatingFlow's white label reporting system allows you to deliver professional, branded analytics to your clients. All reports display your agency branding and can be automated to send on a recurring schedule.

<Info>
  Reports are generated with your custom logo, colors, and domain—your clients see only your brand, not RatingFlow.
</Info>

## Key metrics included

### Review collection metrics

Track the effectiveness of your client's review generation efforts:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Total reviews collected">
    The number of 4-5 star reviews successfully redirected to public platforms (Google, Facebook, Trustpilot).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review request performance">
    Open rates, click rates, and completion rates for SMS and email review requests.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="QR code scans">
    Number of customers who scanned QR codes and completed the rating process.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Platform breakdown">
    Distribution of reviews across Google Maps, Facebook, and other platforms.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Reputation protection metrics

Show clients how you're protecting their online reputation:

- **Negative feedback intercepted:** Count of 1-3 star ratings diverted to private forms
- **Public reputation saved:** Estimated impact on average star rating if negative reviews went public
- **Response rate:** Percentage of private feedback that received a business response
- **Issue resolution:** Tracking of resolved vs. unresolved customer complaints

### SEO and business impact

Demonstrate the value of reputation management:

  <Card title="Local SEO impact" icon="map-location-dot">

## Report customization

### Branded report templates

All reports automatically include:
- Your agency logo in the header
- Your brand colors throughout charts and graphs
- Your agency contact information in the footer
- Custom domain in URLs (e.g., `reviews.youragency.com`)

### Custom metrics

Highlight specific KPIs based on client goals:
- Industry-specific benchmarks
- Competitive comparisons (if data available)
- Custom goal tracking
- ROI calculations

<Tip>
  Add your own insights and recommendations to automated reports to demonstrate strategic value beyond just data.
</Tip>

## Visual report elements

### Charts and graphs

Reports include professional visualizations:

- **Trend lines:** Review volume over time
- **Bar charts:** Platform-by-platform breakdown
- **Pie charts:** Rating distribution (5-star, 4-star, etc.)
- **Comparison graphs:** Month-over-month or year-over-year trends

### Data tables

Detailed tables show:
- Individual review details (date, platform, rating)
- Campaign performance metrics
- Negative feedback log with response status
- QR code performance by location

## Multi-client reporting

As an agency managing multiple clients, you also get consolidated reporting:

### Agency overview dashboard

See all client performance in one place:
- Total reviews generated across all clients
- Combined revenue impact
- Top performing clients
- Clients needing attention

### Comparative analytics

Benchmark clients against each other:
- Review volume by industry
- Response rate comparisons
- Identify best practices from top performers

<Warning>
  Multi-client reports are for internal agency use only. Individual clients only see their own data, ensuring privacy and confidentiality.
</Warning>

## Demonstrating ROI

Use reports to show clients the tangible value of reputation management:

### Reputation protection value

Calculate the estimated impact of negative reviews that were prevented:
- Average star rating without filtering vs. with filtering
- Potential revenue loss from damaged reputation
- Customer relationships saved through private resolution

### SEO and visibility gains

Track improvements in local search performance:
- Google Business Profile views
- Map pack appearances
- Website clicks from Google
- Phone calls generated

### Business growth metrics

Correlate reputation improvements with business outcomes:
- Increased conversion rates
- Higher average order values
- Improved customer retention
- New customer acquisition

<Info>
  Showing clear ROI helps justify your pricing and increases client retention. Include these metrics in monthly reports to reinforce the value you provide.
</Info>

## Report sharing options

### Email delivery

Automated PDF reports can be sent to:
- Primary client contact
- Multiple stakeholders
- Client's clients (for franchises or multi-location businesses)

### Dashboard access

Clients can log in anytime to view:
- Current metrics
- Historical trends
- Downloadable reports

All dashboard access is protected by login credentials and displays your agency branding.

### White labeled embeds

Embed live statistics into:
- Client's internal dashboards
- Your agency client portal
- Presentation slides
