## Perfect for your business type

### Restaurants and cafes

Place QR codes on receipts, tables, or at the counter. Diners can scan and review instantly on their mobile devices, making it easy to capture feedback while the experience is fresh.

### Retail stores

Generate custom QR codes for your front desk or checkout area. Shoppers can quickly share their experience before they leave your store.

### Service businesses

Send automated review requests via SMS or email after each appointment or service completion. No more chasing clients manually.

<Tip>
Use QR codes at physical locations for instant mobile reviews. Perfect for receipts, tables, and front desks.
</Tip>

## Key features for local businesses

### Automated review requests

Stop chasing clients manually. Send bulk requests via SMS/Text and Email, streamlining the process to get more Google reviews with zero friction.

Generate custom QR codes that allow customers to scan and review instantly on their mobile devices. Perfect for restaurants, hotels, and retail stores.

Showcase your success with embeddable widgets. Display your latest 4 and 5-star ratings directly on your website to increase conversion rates and trust with potential buyers.

### Boost local SEO

A higher volume of positive reviews directly correlates to higher rankings in the "Local Pack" and Google Maps, helping potential customers find you first.
