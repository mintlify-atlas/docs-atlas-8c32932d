## Build trust with prospective patients

Your online reputation is critical in healthcare. Prospective patients research doctors, dentists, and medical practices online before booking appointments. RatingFlow helps you systematically collect positive reviews while managing negative feedback privately.

<Info>
Healthcare providers with higher star ratings attract more new patients and build stronger trust in their communities.
</Info>

## How RatingFlow works for healthcare

RatingFlow creates a smart-routing system that protects your practice's reputation while giving patients a voice:

## Key benefits for healthcare providers

### Attract more patients

A strong online reputation helps you stand out in local search results. Higher star ratings lead to more appointment bookings and increased patient trust.

### Address concerns privately

When a patient has a negative experience, RatingFlow gives you the opportunity to address their concerns privately before they leave a public review. This allows you to "save" the patient relationship and potentially resolve issues.

### Boost local search visibility

A higher volume of positive reviews directly correlates to higher rankings in Google's "Local Pack" and Google Maps, helping prospective patients find your practice first.

<Tip>
Send automated review requests via email or SMS after appointments to capture feedback while the experience is fresh in patients' minds.
</Tip>

## Features designed for healthcare practices

### Automated review requests

Stop chasing patients manually for reviews. Send bulk requests via SMS and Email after appointments, streamlining the process with zero friction.

### QR codes for waiting rooms

Place QR codes in your waiting room, at the front desk, or on appointment reminder cards. Patients can scan and review instantly on their mobile devices.

### Website widgets for social proof

Showcase your latest 4 and 5-star ratings directly on your practice website. This builds trust with prospective patients who are researching your services.

### Private feedback management

Handle sensitive patient concerns through private channels, allowing you to respond professionally and personally without public exposure.
