## Features built for agencies

### White-label branding

Rebrand the entire platform with your agency's identity. Your clients will see your logo, colors, and domain throughout their experience.

### Automated review requests

Your clients can send bulk requests via SMS and Email, making it easy to collect reviews at scale without manual effort.

### QR code generation

Perfect for clients with physical locations like restaurants, hotels, and retail stores. Generate custom QR codes that customers can scan to leave reviews instantly.

### Website widgets

Help your clients increase conversion rates by showcasing their latest 4 and 5-star ratings directly on their websites with embeddable widgets.

### Smart routing system

The platform automatically segments customer sentiment before it goes public:
- **Happy customers (4-5 stars):** Redirected to public platforms like Google Maps, Facebook, or Trustpilot
- **Unhappy customers (1-3 stars):** Diverted to a private form for direct resolution
