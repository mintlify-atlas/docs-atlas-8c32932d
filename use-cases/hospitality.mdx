## Turn happy guests into public advocates

In the hospitality industry, your online reputation directly impacts booking rates and revenue. RatingFlow helps hotels, resorts, and hospitality businesses systematically collect positive reviews while managing negative feedback privately.

<Info>
Hospitality businesses with higher star ratings on Google Maps, TripAdvisor, and booking platforms see significantly higher occupancy rates and revenue per room.

## Key benefits for hospitality businesses

### Increase bookings and revenue

A higher star rating means more bookings. Travelers trust online reviews, and properties with 4.5+ stars generate significantly more revenue than competitors with lower ratings.

### Capture feedback at checkout

Use QR codes at the front desk, on room cards, or in post-stay emails to make it easy for guests to share their experience while it's fresh in their minds.

### Protect your brand reputation

Address guest concerns privately before they become permanent negative reviews. This gives your team the opportunity to "save" the guest relationship and resolve issues personally.

### Boost visibility in local search

A higher volume of positive reviews directly correlates to higher rankings in Google's "Local Pack" and Google Maps, helping travelers discover your property first.

<Tip>
Place QR codes at checkout or include them in post-stay emails to capture reviews while the guest experience is still fresh.
</Tip>

## Features designed for hospitality

### QR code review management

Perfect for hotels and resorts. Generate custom QR codes for front desks, room cards, or common areas that allow guests to scan and review instantly on their mobile devices.

### Automated review requests

Send bulk requests via SMS and Email to guests after checkout. Automate the entire process with zero manual follow-up required from your staff.

### Website widgets and social proof

Showcase your latest 4 and 5-star ratings directly on your booking website. This builds trust with prospective guests and increases conversion rates.

### Multi-location management

If you manage multiple properties, you can handle all locations from a single dashboard while maintaining separate review funnels for each.
